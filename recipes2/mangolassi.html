Mango Lassi

Description

Mango Lassi is a popular dessert drink from the Indian Sub-continent and is a summer special loved by everyone. Mangoes, yogurt, milk/cream, ground cardamoms and a little sweetener will take you to mango heaven!
One sip of a well-made Mango Lassi & you will surely fall in love with it for the amazing flavor and a smooth, creamy texture.

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups mangoes-peeled,seeded,chopped and chilled
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons confectioners sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, yogurt, milk, cream, confectioners' sugar, and cardamom in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately
